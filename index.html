What KASIUS sees that you don't.

Blind Spot Engine

Detects what your feeds aren't showing you. Maps the gaps between what you're consuming and what's actually unfolding across the world.

Truth Calibration Engine

Cross-references claims across hundreds of sources in real-time. Surfaces contradiction, confirms convergence, and scores the reliability of what you're seeing.

Personal Impact Flags

Alerts you when world events directly affect your life — your supply chain, your region, your portfolio, your field. Intelligence that's personal, not generic.

Scenario Planning Mode

Model what-if futures based on live intelligence streams. Explore how current events could cascade and prepare for outcomes before they arrive.

Convergence Alerts

Detects when separate events across different domains are actually connected. Surfaces hidden links between geopolitics, markets, technology, and conflict.

Forward Intelligence Layer

Predicts emerging situations before they trend. Identifies weak signals, accelerating patterns, and tipping points that traditional media misses entirely.
